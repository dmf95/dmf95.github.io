On **June 16** I'll be on stage at Databricks' [Data + AI Summit 2026](https://www.databricks.com/dataaisummit/session/lululemon-how-we-modernized-our-audience-segmentation-and-where-its) in San Francisco, co-presenting some of the work my team has been doing at lululemon.

The short version: our CRM data science team runs **PAVE**, an ML platform that scores millions of guest profiles to personalize marketing. And like a lot of real-world ML, the most time consuming part was never really the models - it was everything *around* them. Data scientists were spending their days babysitting deployments instead of building, and good work took far too long to reach production.

So the talk is about how we changed that:

- **parameterized pipelines** (DABS) that anyone on the team can ship,
- an **enterprise feature store** on Unity Catalog,
- **MLflow 3.0** to unify how we track and promote models,
- and where we think **agentic AI** takes all of this next.

I'm genuinely excited - and a little humbled - to share it on a stage like this. If you'll be at the Summit, please come say hi. And if you can't make it, I'll post takeaways here afterward.

Either way, I'd love to [connect](https://www.linkedin.com/in/domenicfayad/).
